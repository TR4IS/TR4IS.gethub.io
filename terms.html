01 Overview

What This Is

Reachflow is a network of developers. Each member gets a public-facing space and access to free tools — all hosted under their GitHub identity.

By using any part of reachflow — spaces, downloads, or the editor — you agree to everything on this page. If you disagree with any part, stop using the service.

03 Spaces

Developer Spaces

Each developer gets a public page at reachflow.site/spaces/[username]. Spaces are fully customizable with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — what you build is yours.

Everything published in a space is committed to a public GitHub repository. Your content is open and visible to anyone on the internet.

  • Your space runs sandboxed in an iframe — isolated from the main site and other spaces.
  • You keep full ownership of your creative content.
  • Spaces are static HTML only — no server-side code, databases, or persistent backends.
  • Spaces are a free service. We may modify, limit, or discontinue hosting at any time.
  • Do not use your space to impersonate another person or claim a false identity.
  • Do not embed content that violates the rules in section 04.
04 Rules

What's Not Allowed

The following content will get your space removed immediately with no warning, across both spaces and any submitted content.

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) — zero tolerance. Reported to authorities and GitHub.
  • Illegal content — piracy, fraud, hacking tools targeting others.
  • Hate speech, threats, or targeted harassment of any person or group.
  • Spam, phishing, or malware distribution.
  • Content that infringes copyright, trademarks, or intellectual property.
  • Impersonation of any person or organization.
  • Anything designed to deceive, manipulate, or cause harm.
05 Enforcement

How We Handle It

The site owner reserves the right to remove any space or content, at any time, with or without notice, for any violation of these terms.

  • Reachflow is curated — membership is a privilege, not a right.
  • Serious violations may be reported to GitHub and relevant authorities.
  • There is no formal appeals process. Owner discretion is final.
06 Privacy

What We Store

Reachflow stores only what GitHub provides at sign-in: your username, display name, bio, and avatar.

  • No passwords. No emails. No tracking cookies.
  • Your space content lives in a public GitHub repository.
  • Anyone can view or fork that repository — it's open source.
