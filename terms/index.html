03 ScamGuard

Discord Bot

ScamGuard is a Discord bot that monitors messages in servers where it is invited. It scans messages for known scam patterns, phishing URLs, and suspicious invite links.

  • The bot only reads messages in servers where it has been explicitly added by a server admin.
  • No message content is stored or logged by the bot beyond what Discord itself retains.
  • The bot does not collect personal information about Discord users.
  • ScamGuard is provided as-is. Detection is not guaranteed to be 100% accurate.
  • Do not add the bot to servers with the intent to abuse, spam, or test against real users without their knowledge.
